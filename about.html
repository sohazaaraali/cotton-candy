About Sugar Swirls by Soha

Meet Soha

Hi! I'm Soha, a middle school student at Grisham Middle School in Canyon Creek, Austin, TX with a passion for creating magical, sweet experiences. Sugar Swirls by Soha is my small business dedicated to bringing joy and delicious cotton candy to celebrations and events.

Our Story

What started as a fun project has grown into a beloved local business! I noticed that everyone loves cotton candy—it brings back childhood memories and adds a special touch to any occasion. With a commitment to quality ingredients and creative flavors, I decided to share my passion with my community.

Our Mission

To create fresh, delicious cotton candy that makes every moment sweeter. Whether it's a birthday party, family event, or community gathering, Sugar Swirls by Soha is here to add smiles and sweetness to your special days.

Why Choose Us?

  • Freshly Spun: Made to order for maximum freshness and fluffiness
  • Creative Flavors: Classic favorites and unique flavor combinations
  • Quality: Made with care and attention to detail
  • Customizable: Perfect for any event size—from small gatherings to large celebrations
  • Local: Supporting a young entrepreneur in your community
