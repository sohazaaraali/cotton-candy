Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know

How do I place an order? +
Visit our Order page, select your flavors and quantities, fill in your details, and submit. We'll confirm your order by email!
How far in advance should I order? +
We recommend ordering at least 3 days in advance. For large events or party packs, a week's notice is ideal so we can make everything fresh!
Do you deliver or is it pickup only? +
We offer both pickup and local delivery in the Canyon Creek / Austin, TX area. Delivery details can be arranged when you place your order.
Are there any allergens in your cotton candy? +
Our cotton candy is made from sugar and flavoring. It is gluten-free, dairy-free, and nut-free. If you have specific allergy concerns, please reach out to us before ordering.
Can I request custom colors or flavors for my event? +
Absolutely! We love creating custom combinations. Use our Request a Quote page to tell us about your event and we'll work with you on colors and flavors.
